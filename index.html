Who we are

Crazy Panda Village is a play-to-earn MMORPG game based on Solana blockchain. CPV is a multiplayer action game centered around NFTs, Staking and Farming, where players can collect, trade and battle one another.

Each CPV NFT is 100% unique and gives you a way to interact with the fantasy game world. All the NFTs can be inserted into our metaverse.
Each CPV is uniquely created and carefully designed by highly experienced artists.
We believe, that we can revolutionise the industry.
